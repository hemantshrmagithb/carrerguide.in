Science Stream

Private / Professional Courses

Government Jobs (after Class 12 or Graduation track)

  • Defence: NDA (Army, Navy, Air Force) — 12th ke baad entry
  • Paramilitary / CAPF constable & technical roles — 12th ke baad
  • Indian Railways: Group C/D technical & clerk roles (as per notification)
  • SSC (CHSL) — 12th ke baad LDC/DEO/PA/SA
  • State Police: Constable / Technical support roles (as per state)
  • ISRO/DRDO Technician & Assistant (notifications + eligibility vary)
  • Graduation ke baad: SSC CGL, State PSC, Banking (IBPS/PO/Clerk)

Note: Kuch exams 12th ke turant baad, aur kuch graduation ke baad hoti hain — eligibility details check karna zaroori hai.

FAQs — Class 12 ke baad kya?

12th ke turant baad best government jobs kaun si hoti hain?

NDA, SSC CHSL, Railway Group D/C, aur State Police Constable sabse popular options hain jo directly 12th ke baad available hote hain.

Agar graduation karu to kya options badh jaate hain?

Haan, graduation ke baad SSC CGL, Banking (PO/Clerk), UPSC Civil Services, aur State PSC exams khul jaate hain.

Private courses better hain ya government exams?

Ye depend karta hai tumhari interest aur stability preference pe — government exams stable jobs deti hain, jabki private/professional courses career growth aur variety provide karte hain.

Science, Commerce aur Arts me se kiska scope zyada hai?

Tino streams ka apna unique scope hai — Science me technical fields, Commerce me finance & business, aur Arts me civil services & creative fields ka strong scope hota hai.

Fail hone ke baad kya options bache rehte hain?

Reappear karke boards clear karna ek option hai, lekin vocational courses, skill diplomas aur government ITI bhi ek alternative career path provide karte hain.
